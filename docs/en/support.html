Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Is there a limit on the number of conversions?
The free version allows up to 3 conversions per day. You can watch a reward ad to unlock 1 additional conversion. Upgrading to Pro removes all conversion limits.
Q. What features does the Pro version include?
The Pro version offers unlimited conversions and an ad-free experience. A lifetime purchase plan ($4.99) is available.
Q. How do I restore my purchase?
Go to the settings screen in the App and tap "Restore Purchases." Your previous purchases will be restored if you are signed in with the same Apple ID.
Q. What video formats are supported?
The App supports videos recorded on iPhone (MOV, MP4). Converted videos are saved in MP4 format.
Q. Where are converted videos saved?
Converted videos are saved to your device's photo library.
Q. Is my video data sent externally?
No. All video conversion processing is performed on your device. Video data is never transmitted to external servers.
